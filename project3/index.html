QUIKKSTEP

By Dean Flint

How to Play

  • Up/Down Arrow Keys to quickly jump vertically up or down to avoid oncoming obstacles
  • You move automatically from left to right, obstacles always move towards you
  • You bounce back in the opposite direction when hitting one end of the screen
  •  
  • "Score" is gained by successfully avoiding obstacles
  • "Intensity" determines your speed, how quickly obstacles appear, and your Score multiplier
  • Intensity increases every 20 seconds
  •  
  • See how long you can last!
