Project 3 Proposal

Title: QUIKKSTEP

Concept: An auto-runner styled game where the player character runs from side-to-side across the horizontal plane, bouncing back in the opposite direction when they reach the opposite end of the screen. The player has obstacles they are meant to dodge sent at them in the opposing direction, and the player is meant to dodge them by "quickstepping" (snapping up or down to another preset y-level) out of the way. It's a high score oriented game, with a scaling difficulty method of throwing more projectiles at the player over time, but with increasing the score rewarded as it gets more difficult.

Genres: Endless Runner, Action

Platform: Desktop Web

Story: No real story, kinda just abstract

Aesthetics: Unsure yet, but maybe something super minimal and abstract and dystopian, maybe like The Cube movie from 1997 but way simpler. Just spitballing though

Gameplay: The player will have no control over their horizontal movement, but will be able to control themselves on the vertical axis by snapping themselves to one of a set of predefined y-axes.
