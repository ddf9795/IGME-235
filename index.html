Dean Flint's 235 Page

✨ Hiya! I'm Dean! ✨

As one might expect from a GDD student, I thoroughly immerse myself in everything video games. I am ALWAYS looking to experience brave, novel ideas, find new ways to experience and reframe the old, and taking inspiration from everywhere and everything to formulate my own ideas, regardless of genre. I want to make unique experiences which deviate from the status quo and make the player question the limits of a genre and of games in general, with the sole goal to elevate the beauty of video games as an art form.By the time I graduate, I want to be able to have the knowledge and the tools to inspire others and, hopefully one day, man my own indie studio.
