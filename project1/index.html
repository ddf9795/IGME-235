Leftie's Cannoneer Class + More!

tModLoader 1.4.3

A functionally feature-complete mod for Terraria's tModLoader v1.4.3 that adds:

  • 1 new gun-type weapon made for the pre-EoW section of progression, with 2 new early bullet types to match
  • 1 new Ranged weapon type, the Cannon, with 15 unique weapons and 9 matching types of cannonball ammunition
  • A new lategame armor piece, the Shroomite Pirate Hat, made to buff cannon damage
  • 5 new accessories which augment the power of bullets and cannonballs
  • 1 new NPC, the Reclaimer, which rewards fighting past bosses with the opportunity to reobtain items from complex crafting trees
  • 1 new food item and 1 new crafting item

Functionally complete, only unreleased due to a lack of unique sprites relating to the Reclaimer NPC.
